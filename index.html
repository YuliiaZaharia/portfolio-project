Our History

2022 - Edge is established and features security consultancy. 2021 - Rebranding under The Holding as a visionary trajectory towards the future. 2020 - Extell is established to feature innovative custom security and safety solutions. 2018 - Alali Holding was formed in August 2018 as a private company to manage their subsidiaries. 2016 - Technolab was created with a focus on education technology and ERM services. 2013 - Q-Tec Medical was established with a focus on medical supplies. 2010 - Q-Tec Switchgear Factory was created seeing Q-Tec as a major source for electrical contracting business. 1998 - Q-Tec Air Conditioning Factory was established under the patronage of Q-Tec Group. 1997 - Sheikh Abdulrahman Jassim A J Al-Thani organized Q-Tec Group.
